About Me


I live simply. If I can laugh heartily, share delicious meals with loved ones (I love food!), and go on adventures, doing so with kindness, that is happiness for me.

Most of my free time is spent with friends, family, and my dog - enjoying movies/Netflix, escape rooms, game nights, traveling, and eating (have I mentioned my love for food yet??).
